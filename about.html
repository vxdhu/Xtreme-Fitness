Our Story

Fresh off his victory at the 2011 Russian WPC Championships, powerlifting champion Ganesh Pathak had a vision – to build a training ground where raw potential meets world-class discipline. That year, Xtreme Fitness was born: a humble academy dedicated to forging champions of all ages.

From dusty lifting platforms to podium finishes, we’ve spent 13 years turning grit into gold. Our students have hauled home state medals, national titles, and international acclaim – each victory proving that greatness isn’t inherited, it’s built.

Today, we’ve expanded into a full-scale gym and martial arts academy, but our core remains unchanged: to unleash the competitor in every student – whether they’re chasing trophies or personal triumphs.

This is where legends train. This is Xtreme Fitness.

We’re a community dedicated to helping you achieve your fitness and martial arts goals. With state-of-the-art facilities, expert trainers, and a wide range of programs, we provide everything you need to push your limits and succeed.
